### Regulatory Flags Implemented (7)
1. `flag_dti_43` — DTI > 43% (CFPB QM)
2. `flag_lti_high` — LTI > 50%
3. `flag_pti_high` — PTI > 35% (FHA)
4. `flag_predatory` — Low income + high loan
5. `flag_dual_stress` — Multiple stress indicators
6. `flag_debt_burden` — Debt > 50% of income
7. `flag_large_loan_dti` — Large loan on stretched borrower
